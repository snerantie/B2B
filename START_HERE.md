# 👋 START HERE - Quick Overview

## What You Have

A complete **B2B marketplace platform** for connecting African businesses with financial service providers.

**Key Point:** You're **NOT** a service provider. You're the marketplace that connects buyers with sellers and earns commission on deals (like Alibaba or Amazon for B2B fintech).

---

## 🎯 What This Does

### For Businesses (Buyers):
- Post service requirements (RFQs)
- Get multiple competitive quotes
- Compare providers side-by-side
- Negotiate directly with providers
- Track orders

### For Service Providers (Sellers):
- List their services
- Browse available RFQs
- Submit competitive quotes
- Message potential clients
- Manage engagements

### For You (Platform Owner):
- Earn **5-10% commission** on successful deals
- Subscription revenue from premium provider listings
- Lead generation fees
- Advertising revenue
